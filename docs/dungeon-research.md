# WoW Classic dungeon research notes

The Classic Dungeon Atlas covers all 20 dungeon entries commonly listed for World of Warcraft Classic. Multi-wing hubs are represented as combined entries with `subDungeons` metadata:

- Scarlet Monastery: Graveyard, Library, Armory, Cathedral.
- Maraudon: Purple/Wicked Grotto, Orange/Foulspore Cavern, Inner/Earth Song Falls.
- Dire Maul: East, West, North.
- Stratholme: Live and Undead.
- Blackrock Spire is split into Lower Blackrock Spire and Upper Blackrock Spire because Classic treats UBRS as a 10-player dungeon with Seal of Ascension access.

Each in-app dungeon record includes route, bosses, loot, group tips, quests/prep, group composition and time planning. The text is an original compact planning summary informed by common Classic dungeon guide structures rather than copied guide text.
